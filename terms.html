Terms, Payment & Refund Policy

Effective date: June 10, 2026

Services

Zax Services LLC provides local service coordination, property support, cleanout and hauling coordination, grounds support, facility-support coordination, and related practical service work in South Carolina. Work is accepted only when the scope is lawful, safe, and appropriate for Zax Services LLC or qualified subcontractor support.

Quotes and scope

Prices are provided by written quote or invoice. Quotes may include labor, materials, travel, disposal, subcontractor costs, scheduling costs, and job-specific fees. A quote is not final until accepted by the customer and confirmed by Zax Services LLC.

Payment terms

Payment is due according to the approved quote or invoice. Deposits may be required for materials, subcontractor scheduling, disposal, or other upfront costs. Online payments may be processed through a third-party payment provider.

Cancellations

If a customer cancels before work begins, refundable amounts will be returned minus approved materials, disposal, travel, subcontractor, scheduling, or other unrecoverable costs already incurred for the job.

Refunds and corrections

Completed service work is generally not refundable. If there is a concern with completed work, customers should contact Zax Services LLC promptly. Correction requests will be reviewed in good faith based on the approved scope, job conditions, and documentation.

Licensed or regulated work

Zax Services LLC does not present itself as a licensed electrical, plumbing, HVAC, structural, environmental, or other regulated trade provider unless explicitly stated in a written quote and performed by a properly qualified party. Jobs requiring licensed or specialized work may be referred, declined, or coordinated with qualified subcontractors.
