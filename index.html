Payment & service policies

These policies keep expectations clear for customers and payment processing.

Pricing

Pricing is provided by quote or invoice. Quotes may include labor, materials, subcontractor costs, travel, disposal fees, and other job-specific costs when applicable.

Payment

Payment terms are listed on the approved quote or invoice. Deposits may be required for materials, scheduling, or subcontractor costs before work begins.

Cancellations & refunds

If a job is cancelled before work begins, refundable amounts are returned minus approved materials, disposal, travel, subcontractor, or other unrecoverable costs. Completed service work is generally not refundable, but correction requests will be reviewed in good faith.

Privacy

Customer information is used to review, quote, schedule, invoice, and complete requested services. Zax Services LLC does not sell customer contact information.
